Closet End

My biggest project - On going

Closet End is a dark puzzle RPG game. In it, you will control Chloé with her teddy bear friend called Oursi.
Together, they will fight against the horrible ideas that Chloé has from the exterior world because of her agoraphobia.
Her goal is to get out of this nightmare and be able to find a quiet and nice life.
Is she the only child suffering of this? You will know that by playing the game that is still in development!
Musics are really important in this game and they are all made by Romar1!
